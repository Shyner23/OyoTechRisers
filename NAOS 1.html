Our Vision

To raise tech moguls in Oyo Town who are able to restore the glory of the town that has been lost for years.

Our Mission

NaosTechrise is a student-driven initiative focused on promoting technology awareness, skill development, and innovation in Oyo State. We believe technology can restore the pride of our historic town and empower the youth.

What We Offer


  • Free coding bootcamps
  • Tech mentorships and internships
  • Workshops on AI, Robotics, Data Analytics, and Web Development
  • Community tech challenges and hackathons
  • Graphics design[UI/UX]
  • Video Editing and contentCreation
